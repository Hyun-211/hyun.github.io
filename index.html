I am a Ph.D. student in theoretical physics at The University of Tokyo, supervised by Prof. Jun'ichi Yokoyama, and a JSPS Research Fellow (DC1). I study gravity from both phenomenological and theoretical perspectives. My research began with inflationary cosmology, with a particular focus on the observational consistency of the R² inflationary model, reheating, and baryogenesis. Recently, I have been exploring scattering amplitudes and their applications to black hole physics, including quantum effects for black holes, Hawking radiation, and gravitational memory.

You can find my papers on INSPIRE-HEP.
